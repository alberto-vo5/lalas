At Lala’s Gorditas, the safety of our guests and employees is our top priority every day. While health and safety standards are followed on a daily basis, we wanted to make you aware of the extra precautions we are taking during this time of heightened concern about the corona virus.

PRECAUTIONS WE ARE TAKING:

  1. We are monitoring closely CDC recommendations and updates
  2. We are sanitizing all surfaces and handles in our kitchen throughout the day.
  3. We are sanitizing the order counter and pick up window, and our hands after every transaction, with bleach water.
  4. We have retrained all our employees on proper hand washing procedures, which means we wash our hands with soap and warm water for at least 20 seconds, throughout the day.
  5. We are providing disinfecting wipes at our outdoor tables for our guests use.
  6. We are wiping down our guest tables with sanitizing solution.
  7. We are not shaking hands, and limiting our personal contact with each other per CDC guidelines.
  8. Remember you can go to our website, lalasgorditas.com, and from the link on our home page, order and pay on line. If you do, give us a call when you arrive at Lala’s, and we will bring your order to your car!!!!
